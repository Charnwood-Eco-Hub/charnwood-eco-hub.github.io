# Charnwood Eco Hub Tabletop Sale

Join us on Saturday 4th July 2026 from 10am-2pm at Charnwood Eco Hub,
SOFA Building, Clarence Street, Loughborough, LE11 1DY for our first
*Tabletop Sale*. It's free, and no need to book.

**We’re having a clear out!**

Come and rummage through a treasure trove of tools, homeware, books, eco
bits, and all sorts of useful odds and ends looking for a new home.

*Bargains, surprises, and sustainable shopping galore — because one
person’s surplus is another person’s inspiration!*
