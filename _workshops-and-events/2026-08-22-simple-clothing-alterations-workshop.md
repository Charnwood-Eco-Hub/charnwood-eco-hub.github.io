# Charnwood Eco Hub Simple Clothing Alterations Workshop

Join us on Saturday 22nd August 2026 from 11am-1pm at Charnwood Eco Hub, 7-8 Baxter Gate, Loughborough, LE11 1TG for a simple clothing alterations workshop.

*Refresh your wardrobe sustainably by learning essential clothing alteration techniques to extend the life of your favourite garments.*

You can book your place by scanning the QR code on the poster or [click
here](https://www.eventbookings.com/b/event/simple-clothing-alterations-workshop). The
workshop costs £5 per person for students, low income, retired or Eco Hub
members, £10 per person standard price.
