# Charnwood Eco Hub Upcycled Inner Tube Jewellery Workshop

Join us on Saturday June 13th 2026, from 11am-1pm at Charnwood Eco Hub,
SOFA Building, Clarence Street, Loughborough, LE11 1DY

**Get creative and give new life to old materials at our Inner Tube
Upcycled Jewellery Workshop!**

In this relaxed, hands-on session you’ll learn how to transform reclaimed
bicycle inner tubes into bold, stylish jewellery and accessories to take
home. Perfect for beginners and crafty creatives alike, with all materials
supplied by Two Wheel Campus.

You can book your place by scanning the QR code on the poster or [click
here](https://www.eventbookings.com/b/event/inner-tube-jewellery-workshop).
The workshop costs £10 per person for students, low income, retired or Eco Hub
members, £15 per person standard price.
