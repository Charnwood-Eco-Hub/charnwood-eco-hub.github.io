# Charnwood Eco Hub Pride Flag Making Workshop

Join us on Saturday 8th August 2026 from 11am-1pm at Charnwood Eco Hub, 7-8 Baxter Gate, Loughborough, LE11 1TG for a colourful felt flag making workshop.

*Celebrate diversity and self-expression by designing and crafting your own Pride flag in a supportive, inclusive environment.*

The workshop is free, but booking is required. You can book your place by scanning the QR code on the poster or [click here](https://www.eventbookings.com/b/event/pride-flag-making-workshop).
