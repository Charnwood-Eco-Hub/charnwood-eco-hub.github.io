# Charnwood Eco Hub Easter Activity Day

*Come to Charnwood Eco Hub from 10.30AM-1.30PM on Saturday 4th April 2026 and join us for some fun 'make your own' Easter activities using materials from our Scrapstore and our famous Smoothie Bike!*

**Learn how to repurpose materials and create something useful and beautiful for your gardens or other outdoor spaces.**

The activities are suitable for children, but all children must be supervised by adults. Adults are also welcome to take part in the activities, even if they aren't supervised by children!

You can book your place by scanning the QR code on the poster or [here](https://www.eventbookings.com/b/event/easter-activity-day). Tickets for the Easter Activity Day cost £5.
